ABOUT PROLIFE HOSPITAL

Trusted Women's Healthcare in Chennai Since 2013

OUR STORY:

Prolife Hospitals began in December 2013 with a focused mission: deliver specialized women's healthcare to families across West Chennai.

Today, we're recognized as one of the best maternity hospitals in Chennai, serving women from Manapakkam, Mugalivakkam, Porur, Ramapuram, and nearby IT corridors including DLF, HP, L&T, Vishranth Tech Park, Olympia Tech Park, and Tamarai Tech Park.

Over the past decade, we've helped thousands of women through pregnancy, fertility challenges, and gynecological concerns. Our growth comes from one simple principle: treat every patient the way we'd want our own family treated.

What Makes Us Different

Specialized Focus on Women's Health

Unlike general hospitals where obstetrics is just another department, women's healthcare is our entire practice. Our doctors don't split attention across multiple specialties—they've dedicated their careers exclusively to gynecology, obstetrics, and fertility care. This focused expertise means better outcomes for you.

Complete Care in One Location

Stop running between different clinics for consultations, scans, lab tests, and medications. At Prolife, you'll complete everything in a single visit. Your doctor orders an ultrasound? Walk down the hall. Need blood work? Our lab is ready. Prescriptions? Pick them up before you leave. This saves your time and eliminates the frustration of coordinating between providers who never speak to each other.

Your Doctor, Your Entire Journey

When you select an obstetrician at Prolife, they stay with you from your first prenatal visit through delivery and postpartum care. No rotating doctors. No strangers delivering your baby. Your physician knows your medical history, understands your birth preferences, and has built trust with you over nine months. That continuity matters when you need it most.

FAQ

Can I transfer my pregnancy care mid-term?
Yes. Many women join us after poor experiences elsewhere. Bring your medical records, and we'll continue your prenatal care seamlessly.
Will my partner be present during delivery?
Absolutely. Partners can stay throughout labor and delivery, including cesarean sections.
Do you handle emergency deliveries?
Our obstetric team is available 24/7 for labor emergencies and pregnancy complications.
What's your approach to fertility treatment?
We start with simpler interventions before advancing to expensive procedures. Many couples conceive with basic treatment adjustments.
How long is a typical hospital stay?
Natural deliveries: 24-48 hours. Cesarean sections: 3-4 days. We're flexible based on your recovery.
Do you provide second opinions?
Yes. Bring your test results and recommendations from other doctors. We'll give honest feedback.
What age range do you treat?
From adolescence (menstrual concerns, vaccination) through menopause and beyond.
